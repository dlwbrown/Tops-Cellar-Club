# Tops Cellar Selection — Member Guide

Welcome to the Club. Here's everything your app can do.

## Getting started

- **Installing the app:** Scan the in-store QR code (or open topscellarclub.co.za), then tap **Share → Add to Home Screen**. The app opens full-screen, like a normal app.
- **Joining:** Tap **Become a member**, fill in your details and taste preferences, and you're in. You'll get a membership number and digital card.
- **Alerts:** Turn on notifications when asked so you never miss your Discovery Box, events, and member-only pricing.

## Home

Your home screen shows your membership at a glance, this month's highlights, and **Quick actions** to jump anywhere — Discovery Box, Magazine, Events, Specials, Rate wines, and your card.

The **bell** (top right) opens your notifications.

## Discover & wines

- Browse the wine catalogue from **Discover**.
- Tap any wine to see its story, tasting notes, food pairings, serving temperature and rating.
- **Search** for a wine by name, producer, region or style.

## Favourites

- On a wine's page, tap the **heart** to save it to your Cellar.
- Tap it again to remove it. Your favourites appear under **Cellar → Favourites**.

## Rating wines

- On a wine's page, tap **Add my rating**, choose 1–5 stars, add an optional note, and **Save**.
- Your ratings live under **Cellar → Ratings**, and they help set each wine's average score.

## The Cellar

Your personal space, with two tabs:

- **Favourites** — wines you've hearted.
- **Ratings** — wines you've reviewed.

## Magazine

Tap **Magazine** (on Home) for the Club's editorial — **Articles, Promotions, Wine Education, News and Seasonal features**. Filter by category at the top and tap any piece to read it.

## AI Sommelier

Tap **Sommelier** and ask anything — "What wine goes with a braai?", "A crisp white under R120?" — for instant, personalised recommendations based on your taste preferences. It only ever suggests wines that are **actually in stock**, and shows each one as a card with its bottle, **live price**, variety, region and food pairing — tap a card to open the full wine.

## Membership card

Tap **Card** for your digital membership card and personal **QR code**. Show it in store to be recognised as a member.

## Discovery Box

Each month's Discovery Box is a hand-picked selection of **wines**. It appears on the home screen — tap it to see what's inside and either **join the priority list** or **reserve** it, depending on the season.

## Events

Browse upcoming tastings and dinners under **Events**, and tap **RSVP** to book your seat.

## Member Specials

**Specials** shows member-only pricing. Tap a special's image to enlarge it.

## Notifications

When the Club sends news — a new box, an event, a special — it appears as a push notification and in your in-app feed (the bell on home).
